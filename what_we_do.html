Design & Construction

Our team provides comprehensive design and construction services, including working with contractors to ensure our client’s vision for the product is accomplished while minimizing construction impacts on stakeholders and airport operations. From front-end project development services to detailed design engineering - our team’s concentration on the aviation industry allows us to deliver results with greater efficiency while leveraging leading industry processes.

02

Planning Services

Aviation planning provides a roadmap for an airport’s future. We bring a practical, cost-effective approach to identify and prioritize needs, evaluate options, and help sponsors develop forward-thinking strategies.

For nearly 30 years, we have assisted several clients with airport planning. Whether you are updating technology, wanting to be more sustainable, or expanding your airport to serve larger aircraft, our engineers will advise and assist throughout the process.

How We Support

Develop Airport Capital Improvement Program (ACIP) packages for our client’s annual submittals

Prepare exhibits for presentations to local & state governments

Attend meetings with the county, local, state, & federal elected officials to assist clients with presentations to request funding for proposed Airport projects

Attend meetings with future tenants, as requested by our clients

Assist our clients with the preparation of pre-applications and applications for federal/state assistance

03

Assistance With Obtaining Funds

An added unique service provided by TBE is our comprehensive grant administration and project financial management. The relationships we have cultivated with state and federal agencies have helped us develop an extremely knowledgeable staff familiar with all aspects of grant management

Our firm prepares grant request forms, reimbursement requests, and final grant closeout documentation on our client’s projects. TBE’s assistance with these grant administration duties enables us to assist our clients in maintaining good steward status with the FAA.
